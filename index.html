Why Choose Us ?

Quality Cuisine

You are what you eat and we make sure it's nothing but the best. BellView offers cuisines from all across the globe at its partner restaurants so you don't run out of options when exploring great taste. We make sure that you get the best of what your locality has to offer. From South Indian to Chinese and Italian to every cuisine that is there in the world, we provide you with the list of the menu right on your smartphone screen; accompanied with not only prices but the complete nutritional benefits, so you can choose what is right for your body and go easy on your pocket.

Fresh Food

Are you certain that the food you order is fresh? Well, at Bellview, you are. From plucking the recently-harvested vegetables and freshly carve up meat to dressing the main course and sweetening your desserts, our team ensure that the food you're served is up to the mark by gingerly selecting our partners. We employ routined procedures to test the quality of stock as well as the finished product because there is no compromise when it comes to eating healthy.

Friendly Staff

Skill and amiability are the qualities to possess and we have a staff who excel in both. From creating a warm-hearted environment to providing customer support, we got it all covered. BellView introduces the modern era of Chef-Assistants who provide you with your quality cuisine in lesser time, which was next to impossible in the conventional waiter approach. While our QR-codes get your orders as soon as you enter, our Chef-Assistants will provide you with the chef-created deliciousness in no time. In case you need a hand with the technology, they come equipped with tech support for added benefits.

Go Queue-less

Forgot to make reservations? Tired of waiting for the waiter to make an order? We've all been there but now, we decide to break through. Bellview presents an innovative way of hotel management, where waiting in queues is a thing of past. Our new and improved model introduces QR-codes which you can scan upon entering the restaurant and book a table within the premises of your favourite restaurant. Our enhanced menu software will show you the dishes with availability, prices and nutritional values while you can make the payment straight from the table.
It's new. It's convenient. It saves time.

BellView Freebies

At BellView, we care about our customers. And that's why with every meal you eat, we are giving you a chance to get freebies. Every time you scan a QR-code at one of our partner restaurants, you get to avail a free dish or reward points which you can collect to win exclusive deals at BellView associates. We are not done here, because every time you dine with us, we give you access to BellCodes, the enveloped QR-codes you can use to win high-discounts and benefits at our partner establishments. BellCodes can also be traded with friends and relatives for shared benefits.
